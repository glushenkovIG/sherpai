Core Utilities

Social Engineering Protection

In an age where sophisticated social engineering attacks target individuals through digital communications, SherpaAI provides robust protection by detecting patterns, analyzing behavior, and using historical context.

  • Pattern Recognition: Identifies common tactics like urgency, authority exploitation
  • Behavioral Analysis: Detects when contacts behave out of character
  • Real-time Alerts: Provides immediate warnings for high-risk communications
  • Educational Components: Builds resilience through awareness

Protection extends from financial security to account safety, personal security, and specialized protection for vulnerable populations.

Social Abilities Maximization

Beyond protection, SherpaAI helps optimize social interactions and relationship management, addressing modern challenges of digital communication overload.

  • Relationship Analytics: Measures health using scientific frameworks
  • Social Circle Mapping: Visualizes connections and identifies opportunities
  • Strategic Recommendations: Suggests timely check-ins with neglected connections
  • Opportunity Identification: Highlights beneficial connections within your network

From relationship maintenance to communication effectiveness, SherpaAI helps you navigate complex social landscapes.

Built on Telegram-Proxy-Caching-Client

SherpaAI leverages our custom telegram-proxy-caching-client to securely analyze your communications without compromising privacy. All data processing happens locally, ensuring your conversations never leave your control.

This innovative architecture allows for powerful AI capabilities while maintaining complete data sovereignty.

Our Mission

In the age of AI, vast amounts of social data are being weaponized against us. We believe it's time to utilize that data for our benefit instead. Our mission is to create a sovereign data framework where your social interactions work for you, not against you.

After experiencing firsthand how even tech-savvy individuals can fall victim to sophisticated social engineering, we're committed to building protection for everyone. Simultaneously, we're addressing the challenge of maintaining meaningful relationships in our increasingly fragmented digital world.
